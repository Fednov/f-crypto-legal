# Privacy Policy for F_Crypto

**Last Updated: 2026-06-29**

This Privacy Policy describes how **F_Crypto** ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

*   **Personal Data:** We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. No registration is required to use the App.
*   **Local Storage:** Your settings, favorite currencies, and price alerts are stored locally on your device using system-standard storage (*SharedPreferences*). This data is never uploaded to our servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide real-time data and monitor app performance. These services may collect technical information:

*   **Market Data Providers (Binance API, CoinGecko API):** These services provide price information. They may process your IP address as part of standard web requests when the App requests data.
*   **Error Reporting (Sentry):** We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and performance data to improve the App. This may include device model, OS version, and technical logs. We do not send any personal data to Sentry.

## 3. Security

We value your trust in using the App and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your locally stored data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
